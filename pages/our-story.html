At Castle's Custom Homes, our team combines generations of building experience with thoughtful design, strong systems, and a hands-on approach.

Each member plays a defined role in guiding projects from concept to completion, ensuring clear communication, quality craftsmanship, and a smooth experience for our clients. Together, we're committed to building homes that reflect both care and lasting value.

Family Behind It All

What We Do

Design. Build. Deliver.

We design and build custom homes tailored to the way our clients live. From initial planning and budgeting through construction and completion, we guide each project with clear communication, thoughtful decision-making, and hands-on management.

Our focus is creating homes that balance design, function, and long-term value — built with care, precision, and respect for the people who will live in them.

The Foundation

Kyle and Tammy Castle have spent years building more than structures — they've built trust with families across Twin Falls and the Magic Valley. As small business owners themselves, they understand that every project is more than a home. It's a milestone. A legacy. A place where life happens.

From the very beginning, Castle's was founded on a simple conviction: homebuilding should be honest, personal, and rewarding — for the builder and for the family moving in. That hasn't changed. Every home Castle's builds carries the same standard of care that Kyle and Tammy would demand for their own family.

"We started this company because we believed families in the Magic Valley deserved a builder they could actually trust — someone who would be there start to finish, with no surprises."

— Kyle Castle, President

How We Work

From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, you'll work directly with our team. There are no handoffs to strangers mid-build, no lapses in communication. That personal commitment is the foundation of everything Castle's Custom Homes stands for.

Kyle oversees every build from the ground up — bringing both technical precision and creative vision to each project. Tammy ensures the client experience is smooth at every step, from the excitement of design selections through the details of financing. Together, they've built a company where clients feel like partners, not just customers.

"Every family we build for becomes part of our story. We take that seriously — long after the keys are handed over."

— Tammy Castle, Co-Founder

Rooted in the Magic Valley

Twin Falls isn't just where Castle's operates — it's home. Kyle and Tammy are invested in this community in ways that go beyond business. Through Castle's Corner Corp, the family supports local events, youth programs, and the broader Twin Falls business community. When you build with Castle's, you're supporting a company that invests right back into the neighborhood you're building in.

That's what separates Castle's from a national builder or an out-of-town contractor. Every dollar spent here stays here. Every subcontractor is local. Every relationship is built to last.

Kyle & Tammy Castle Founders, Castle's Custom Homes — Twin Falls, Idaho
