About Us

Rooted in the
Magic Valley

We don't just build in this community — we live in it, invest in it, and are proud to call it home.

Our Community

More Than a Builder —
A Neighbor

Castle's Custom Homes was founded in the Magic Valley and has never left. Every home we build, every subcontractor we hire, and every relationship we form stays right here — because this is where we live too.

Hire Local

We Keep Work
in the Valley

Every subcontractor, supplier, and tradesperson we work with is local. We believe that building a home should strengthen the local economy — not send money elsewhere.

From the concrete crews to the finish carpenters, the people building your home are your neighbors. They take pride in the work because they live here too.

Youth & Education

Investing in the
Next Generation

Kyle and Tammy believe in giving young people in the Magic Valley the same opportunities they had. Castle's actively supports youth programs, school initiatives, and vocational education that introduce the next generation to careers in the trades.

The construction industry needs skilled, passionate people — and we're committed to helping build that pipeline right here at home.

Local Events

Showing Up
for Twin Falls

You'll find Castle's at local fairs, sponsoring community events, and supporting organizations that make the Magic Valley a better place to live and raise a family.

We know that a strong community makes for better neighborhoods — and better neighborhoods are what we're in the business of building.

H

Hire Local

Every trade on every job is sourced from within the Magic Valley region whenever possible.

G

Give Back

A portion of every completed project supports local causes — from youth sports to food programs.

B

Buy Local

Materials are sourced from local suppliers first. Keeping dollars local is part of how we build.

S

Stay Local

Castle's has no plans to expand beyond the Magic Valley. This is home — and we're here to stay.
