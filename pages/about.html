Castle's Custom Homes is more than a builder—it's a family-owned business built on relationships, integrity, and a deep love for the Magic Valley.

The People Behind Every Build

Our Team

Castle's Custom Homes is built on the talent, dedication, and craft of a close-knit team that brings expertise to every role — from design and project management to sales, finance, and the finishing details that make a house a home.

Kyle Castle

President | General Contractor

Kyle Castle is the President and General Contractor of Castle's Custom Homes and a second-generation builder with deep roots in the Wood River Valley. Raised in the construction industry, Kyle began working in the field during high school and gained early, hands-on experience through both family and professional building environments.

kyle@castlescustomhomes.com  ·  (208) 309-1547

Tammy Castle

Co-Founder | Operations & Design Oversight

Tammy Castle is the Co-Founder of Castle's Custom Homes and oversees operations, systems, and design direction for the company. She joined the business in 2003, bringing a strong background in business management, financial organization, and client coordination.

tammy@castlescustomhomes.com  ·  (208) 309-1549

Rebecca Binns

Lead Designer

Rebecca Binns serves as Lead Designer at Castle's Custom Homes and plays a key role in shaping the look, feel, and cohesion of each project. With a background rooted in both creative design and construction, Rebecca helps translate client vision into thoughtful, buildable design solutions.

Chayton Castle

Lead Project Manager

Chayton Castle serves as Lead Project Manager at Castle's Custom Homes and represents the third generation of builders in the Castle family. He began working in construction at a young age, gaining hands-on experience in the field throughout high school.

Kylie Spence

Sales & Marketing | Licensed Realtor

Kylie Spence leads sales and marketing for Castle's Custom Homes and serves as the company's licensed Realtor, representing listings throughout both the Wood River and Magic Valleys. She guides buyers through the purchasing process from first showing to closing with clarity and care.

Tyler Spence

Construction Cleans & Staging Support

Tyler Spence supports the Castle's team through construction cleans and staging, ensuring every home is presented at its best before handoff. His work is a key part of delivering the polished, move-in-ready standard that Castle's clients expect.

What Drives Us

Our Values

I

Integrity

We say what we mean and do what we say. No surprises, no runarounds. Honest pricing and honest communication from day one.

C

Craftsmanship

We take pride in every nail, every joint, every finish. Your home deserves to be built as well as it can possibly be built.

C

Community

Twin Falls and the Magic Valley are our home too. We hire locally, support locally, and are invested in the health of this community.

P

Partnership

We treat every client like a long-term partner. Our relationship doesn't end when you get the keys—we're here whenever you need us.

Community

Rooted in the Magic Valley

Beyond building homes, Castle's Corner Corp supports local events, youth programs, and the broader Twin Falls business community. When you build with Castle's, you're investing in a company that invests right back in your neighborhood.
