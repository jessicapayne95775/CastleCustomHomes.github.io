Our Story

Meet
The Castles

Kyle and Tammy Castle have spent years building more than structures—they've built trust with families across Twin Falls and the Magic Valley. As small business owners themselves, they understand that every project is more than a home.

From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, you'll work directly with Kyle and Tammy. That personal commitment is the foundation of everything Castle's Custom Homes stands for.
